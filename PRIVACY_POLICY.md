# Privacy Policy for Income Tax Tracker

**Last updated: January 31, 2026**

## Introduction

Income Tax Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Income Tax Tracker mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you register for our App, we collect:
- Name
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)

### Financial Information
To provide our tax calculation service, we collect:
- Income transaction records
- Transaction amounts and dates
- Transaction descriptions
- Bank names

### SMS Messages
With your permission, our App reads SMS messages to automatically detect bank transaction alerts. We only read messages that match bank alert patterns and do not store or transmit your SMS messages.

### Photos and Files
You may upload photos of bank alerts. These images are processed to extract transaction information and are stored securely.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:
- To provide and maintain our tax tracking service
- To calculate your tax obligations based on Nigerian tax laws
- To allow you to track your income and expenses
- To generate reports and export your financial data
- To improve our App and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely on our servers
- Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard encryption
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data
- Financial data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Share your personal or financial data with third parties
- Sell your data to advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes

We may share data only if:
- Required by law or legal process
- Necessary to protect our rights or safety

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Export your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@incometaxtracker.com

## SMS Permission

Our App requests permission to read SMS messages to automatically detect bank transaction alerts. This permission is used solely for this purpose. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
- Email: support@incometaxtracker.com

## Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

## International Data Transfer

Your data may be stored on servers located outside Nigeria. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.
