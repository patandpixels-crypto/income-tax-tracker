# Privacy Policy for Income Tax Tracker

**Last Updated:** January 8, 2026

Income Tax Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
- **Email Address:** Used for account creation and authentication
- **Name:** Used for personalization and transaction matching
- **Password:** Securely hashed and stored for account security

### 1.2 Transaction Data
- **Amount:** Transaction amounts from bank SMS alerts
- **Date:** Transaction dates
- **Bank:** Bank name from SMS sender
- **Description:** Transaction descriptions extracted from SMS
- **Raw SMS Text:** Stored temporarily in app memory, NOT sent to servers

### 1.3 SMS Permissions (Android Only)
- We request permission to READ SMS messages
- **Only bank SMS alerts are read** - We filter SMS by known Nigerian bank sender IDs
- Personal SMS messages are NOT read, accessed, or stored
- Raw SMS content is NOT uploaded to our servers
- Only parsed transaction data (amount, bank, date, description) is sent to our servers

### 1.4 Technical Information
- Device type and operating system
- App version
- API requests and response times (for debugging)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Core Functionality
- **Account Management:** Create and manage your user account
- **Transaction Tracking:** Store and display your income transactions
- **Tax Calculation:** Calculate estimated taxes based on Nigerian tax brackets
- **SMS Parsing:** Automatically extract transaction details from bank SMS alerts

### 2.2 Service Improvement
- Monitor app performance and fix bugs
- Improve transaction parsing accuracy
- Enhance user experience

### 2.3 Security
- Authenticate users and prevent unauthorized access
- Detect and prevent fraudulent activity
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Storage Location
- Data is stored on secure PostgreSQL databases
- Servers are hosted on Render.com with SSL encryption
- All API communications use HTTPS encryption

### 3.2 Security Measures
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
- JWT tokens for secure authentication
- SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
- Regular security updates and monitoring

### 3.3 Data Retention
- Account data: Retained while your account is active
- Transactions: Retained indefinitely unless deleted by you
- Deleted data: Permanently removed from our servers within 30 days

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### 4.1 We Do NOT Share Your Data
- We do NOT sell your personal information
- We do NOT share your data with third parties for marketing
- We do NOT provide your SMS data to anyone
- We do NOT use your data for advertising

### 4.2 Limited Exceptions
We may disclose your information only in these cases:
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or government request
- **Security:** To protect our rights, safety, or property
- **With Your Consent:** If you explicitly authorize us to share

### 4.3 Service Providers
We use these third-party services:

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

## 5. SMS Permissions Disclosure

### 5.1 Why We Need SMS Permissions
SMS permissions are used EXCLUSIVELY for:
- Reading bank transaction SMS alerts
- Automatically extracting transaction details
- Importing credit transactions to your account

### 5.2 What We DO with SMS
- ✅ Read SMS from known Nigerian bank sender IDs only
- ✅ Parse transaction information (amount, bank, date)
- ✅ Store parsed data on our secure servers
- ✅ Display transactions in your dashboard

### 5.3 What We DON'T DO with SMS
- ❌ Read personal or non-bank SMS messages
- ❌ Store raw SMS content on servers
- ❌ Share SMS data with third parties
- ❌ Use SMS for marketing or advertising
- ❌ Access SMS without your explicit action (tapping "Scan SMS")

### 5.4 SMS Permissions Control
- Permissions are requested only when needed
- You can revoke permissions anytime in Android Settings
- App functionality (except SMS scanning) works without SMS permissions

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Control

### 6.2 SMS Permissions

### 6.3 Marketing Communications
